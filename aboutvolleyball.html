ABOUT VOLLEYBALL

Beach volleyball is a team sport played by two teams which is composed of 2 players in each team. They play on a court which is divided by a net.Â  It is a sport that was invented in the United States and now it has a trending popularity. Volleyball is now played all over the world and ranked just behind soccer among its competitive sports.

Objectives/Rules of the game:

  • Constantly send the ball over the net on the opponent side
  • Prevent the ball on landing on the ground on your side. However, try to hit the ball over the net on the opponents court to win a point
  • The game begins when the player at the right back-row serves the ball to the opponent’s side
  • A team is allowed to hit the ball 3 times to return it to the opponent’s court
  • A player hitting the ball consecutively is not permitted, except when attempting to block the ball
  • A team wins a point when they win the rally. A rally is won when the ball touches the ground, when the ball goes out or when a team fails to return the ball
  • When the receiving team wins the rally, they gain a point and the right to serve. They also have to rotate one position clockwise
  • Games are either “Best of 3” or “Best of 5”
  • Games are usually won when the first team to score 25 points with a minimum difference of 2 points advantage. However, in a deciding game, the first team to score 15 points with a minimum of 2 points advantage.
