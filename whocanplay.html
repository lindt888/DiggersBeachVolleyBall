WHO CAN PLAY?

Volleyball is a sport being played worldwide at least once a week. A sport that is fun and safe because is does not require an extreme fitness. Beach volleyball is suitable for men and women of all ages. Thus, this sport can be great for people who want a low impact sport and to get fit without pushing their limits. Beach volleyball can also be good for competition as well, as it may provide exciting and intense entertainment. In case you are starting a new sport, you should see a doctor to know whether you are eligible to do the sport.
